This website has been created mostly out of sentimental fondness, I'm not a hard core gamer any longer.
I'm a curious front-end developer.

There it is, full disclosure. TITAN's game design and graphics hold a magical appeal and this is the reason for the site. Recently my technical curiosity, for WebGL lead me to learn BabylonJs, and recreate tic-tac-toe in 3D. A project to learn that frame work. When that was done I wanted a different project that would present a more complex gameplay, and I remembered TITAN.

My idea was "what if ... TITAN was an interactive 3D masterboard and battlelands?

So I had a look around and discovered a really active comminuty, with amazing implimentations and regular activity, so instead of trying to reinvent the wheel, I have resoted to spend my time on more graphical interpretations, using Blender and low poly models, to see what TITAN might have looked like now.

But TITAN is very seductive, and complex and a phenominal work of design and it has taken ages to systematically recreate the board, battle fields and legion creatures, which are required for the 3D, so I give these to you, and perhaps you will find them valuable.
