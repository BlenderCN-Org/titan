The extension is a one trick pony, it overlays the ACTS view battle page with graphics, inspired by the original TITAN terrains, from the borad game.
A fresh unit, underlined by player color Casualies, as displayed by ACTS A dead unit, awaiting retaliation
Clicking a unit, on the View Battle page, will present their respective action page, aka the View Battle Creature, where you can assign damage and location.

Clicking a creature while on the View Battle Creature page will return to the View Battle page.

IMPORTANT: This extension does not use Google Analytics, or any form of tracking, out of respect for your privicy.

So if you want more information or encounter a bug, or have a feature request, please visit my Github page for this extension.
